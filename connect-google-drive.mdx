---
title: 'Connect Google Drive'
description: 'Copy files to and from your Lab'
---

Navigate to the Connect Google Drive page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Drive">
    Click the ```Connect``` button.

    Select the Google account you would like to connect.

    Give Agora Labs permission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Folder">
    Click the ```Select Folder``` button.

    Select the folder in your Google drive you would like copy and save files from.

    Click the ```Select``` button on the Drive picker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch your Lab">
    Follow the steps in [Quickstart](quickstart) to launch your Lab. Make sure you toggle on the Drive connection.

    When your Lab is launched, all files in your Google Drive folder will be automatically copied to your lab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy Files">
    At any point in time you can click ```Copy to Drive``` to copy any new files from your Lab to your Drive folder.

    Likewise, you can click ```Copy to Lab``` to copy any new files in your Drive folder to your Lab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close your Lab">
    When you're done with your work, click the ```Close``` button to close your Lab. Any new files on your Lab will be copied to your Drive folder.
    
    <Note>Only files in the Lab subdirectory will be copied.</Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
