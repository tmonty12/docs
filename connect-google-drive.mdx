---
title: 'Connect Google Drive'
description: 'Copy files to and from your Lab'
---

Navigate to the **Google Drive** page.
<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/ConnectGDrivePage.png"
  />`
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Drive">
    Click the ```Connect``` button.

    Select the Google account you would like to connect.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/SelectGAccount.png"
      />
    </Frame>

    <Warning>
      We are currently going through the Google Verification process which takes months to complete. 

      We take data security **seriously** and assure your data is secure. 
    </Warning>

    Click on **Advanced**
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/GoogleNonVerified.png"
      />
    </Frame>

    Click **Go to agoralabs.io**
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/GoToAgora.png"
      />
    </Frame>

    Click **Allow** to give Agora Labs permission to your Drive
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/GiveAgoraPermission.png"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Folder">
    Click the ```Select Folder``` button.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/DriveConnected.png"
      />
    </Frame>

    Select the folder in your Google drive you would like copy and save files from.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/SelectFolderPicker.png"
      />
    </Frame>

    Click the ```Select``` button on the Drive picker.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/SelectedDriveFolder.png"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch your Lab">
    Follow the steps in [Quickstart](quickstart) to launch your Lab. Make sure you toggle on the Drive connection.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/CreateDeploymentDriveEnabled.png"
      />
    </Frame>

    When your Lab is launched, all files in your Google Drive folder will be automatically copied to your lab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy Files">
    At any point in time you can click ```Save to Drive``` to copy any new files from your Lab to your Drive folder.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/CopyFilesButtons.png"
      />
    </Frame>

    Likewise, you can click ```Upload from Drive``` to copy any new files in your Drive folder to your Lab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close your Lab">
    When you're done with your work, click the ```Close``` button to close your Lab. Any new files on your Lab will be copied to your Drive folder.
    
    <Note>Only files in the Lab subdirectory will be copied.</Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
